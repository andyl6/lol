Skills

I only know how to make two dishes. This is a very bad basic rundown of them.

Alfredo

Ingredients: Heavy Cream, Pasta, Garlic, Parmesan, Seasoning, Butter/Oil

  1. Boil water, put in pasta of choice for ~7 min
  2. Grate cheese, cut garlic
  3. When pasta done turn off fire. Put cream and garlic inside different pan
  4. When bubble put in parmesan and then when melted put in seasoning and pasta
  5. Eat








Kimchi Fried Rice

Ingredients: Kimchi, Rice, Meat, Garlic/Onion, Scallion, Oil, Seasoning. Optional; Egg, Cheese, Flakes

  1. Cut garlic/onion, kimchi, scallion, and meat of choice
  2. Oil pan, throw in garlic/onion and scallion until brown. Throw in meat.
  3. Throw in kimchi and rice. Mix, throw in seasonings like salt, sugar, etc.
  4. Optionally make eggs on the side, put in seaweed flakes or cheese.
  5. Yummy
